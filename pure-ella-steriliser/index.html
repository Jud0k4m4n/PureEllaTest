Pure Ella Steriliser

Period hygiene simplified

For optimal intimate hygiene, menstrual cups need to be sterilised before and after menstruation. How do you sterilise your menstrual cup? If you still boil it in a kitchen pot to sterilise it, this will be a life changer product for you that will save you time and hassle.

Safety first. Made of 100% BPA free silicone that stands up to 230C temperature, which makes it safe to use with boiling water. It is also dishwasher friendly, just put it in there to wash after using it.

Sterilised within 2 minutes in the microwave using just water or pouring boiling water from a kettle into the steriliser containing the cup and leaving it with the lid closed for 5 minutes.

Removes all the hassle that comes with sterilising your menstrual cup to give you peace of mind, so that you've got more time to worry about things that matter to you more.

No more compromising your menstrual hygiene while traveling as sterilising your cup won't be an issue anymore. It's small, light and foldable, and also comes with a discreet cotton bag to carry it.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

1) Put the menstrual cups inside the steriliser cup and fill it with water leaving 1 cm space on top

1) Close the lid and microwave it for 2 minutes at maximum power

3) Carefully take out the steriliser cup from the microwave with heat protective gloves and your period cup is nice and clean, ready to be used again

No microwave? Don't worry, you can pour boiling water from a kettle into the steriliser and close it for 5 minutes and your menstrual cup will be sterilised!

If you don't want to compromise your intimate hygiene but you don't like the hassle that comes with sterilising your menstrual cup, Pure Ella steriliser is the product you're looking for!
